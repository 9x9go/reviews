Reviews

This section provides commments on the games of 9 × 9 Go played by 9 Dan professional Go players. The performance of each player is measured by KataGo (v1.12.1; b18nbts550; >200 visits per move) run on iPhone 15 with A Master of Go (v.6.0.1), which is one of the strongest superhuman 9 × 9 Go player at the time of this writing. An old and much weaker version of KataGo topped the chart of Go Quest in 2019.

03/27/23

WGA verified Go AI is the moblie application called A Master of Go, with KataGo engine and the b18nbt neural network that has achieved superhuman performance in 9 × 9 Go. The AI won (2:0) against the old KataGo that topped the charts of human Go servers in 2019, after achieving a 8-Dan ranking on Go Quest, and can predict the next moves of KataTest 9 Dan, with about 95% accuracy. The 5% wrong predictions were the cases where the AI's predicted moves cause higher net scores than the actual moves of KataTest did. The verified AI exploits KataGo which is superhuman at or above 128 visits per move, according to Wang et al. (2023). Read more
